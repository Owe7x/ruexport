MADE IN RUSSIA

On December 10, 2021 , Russian Export Center will host the most useful forum for an exporter - Made in Russia.

This year, together with our partners we have composed the program of the Forum to attract exporters of any scale and with any level of experience. Both a start-up micro-company and a diversified export-oriented holding will enjoy participating in the Forum.

Traditionally, the Forum is attended by top officials of the Government of the Russian Federation. Mikhail Mishustin opened the plenary session in 2020.

The 2021 Forum will be held in a hybrid format. Most of the sessions will be streamed online.

OFFLINE

International Trade Center 12 Krasnopresnenskaya Embankment, Moscow

ONLINE

The broadcast will be available on the website on December 10

PROGRAMME DESCRIPTION

A number of events, that took place over the past few years, will significantly change the global economy and international trade. These events introduce new challenges to all of us.

  • What will be the future of Russian export? What place will it occupy in the global market?
  • What should we focus on, and what factors can be neglected?
  • What needs to be done for the 2030 not to disappoint?

These and many other issues would be addressed at our Forum!
